## 1. What Fauran is (one-liner)

**Fauran** is a proximity-based, **bid-and-book** marketplace that connects people in Pakistan with
**verified** skilled professionals — technicians, plumbers, electricians, AC/appliance repair,
carpenters, painters, and handymen.

- **Name meaning:** *Fauran* (Urdu, فوراً) = "immediately / at once."
- **Tagline (brand-locked, use verbatim):** **"Ab Kam Hoga, Fauran."** ("Now the work gets done — at once.")
- **Category framing:** "Pakistan's bid-and-book services marketplace" / "name your need, pros bid."
- **The model in three words:** **Post → Bid → Book.**
- **Launch cities:** **Lahore & Islamabad** first, then city-by-city.
- **Wordmark:** lowercase **`fauran`**. The logo is a stylized **F with speed/motion lines** (encodes "fast").

## 2. The problem (grounded in PRD §01 + deck slide 2)

Finding a trustworthy plumber, electrician, or AC technician in Pakistan is **slow, informal, and opaque.**
Three concrete pains (use these as the Problem section's three beats):

1. **Finding someone** — "Asking around is the search engine." Whoever picks up the phone first gets the
   job. No way to compare.
2. **Agreeing a price** — Rates are guesswork. Both sides discover the real deal at the front door. No
   price transparency.
3. **When it goes wrong** — No record of the agreement, no reviews, no recourse. You can't hold anyone to
   their word.

For the supply side: skilled professionals have **no reliable channel** to find nearby paying work and are
**undercut by middlemen.**

## 3. The solution — Post → Bid → Book (PRD §01, deck slide 4)

A customer posts what they need with a location and photos. Verified pros **within range** see the job,
place a **bid (price + arrival time)**, and negotiate in chat. The customer **compares bids, ratings, and
distance**, books one, tracks arrival, **confirms completion with a 4-digit code**, pays **cash**, and rates
the work.

**Four steps (use for the Solution section):**
1. **Post** — Say what you need. Add a location, photos, and when you need it.
2. **Bid** — Nearby verified pros send offers: their price and arrival time. Compare, or counter in chat.
3. **Book** — Pick the bid that fits — on price, rating, and distance. Track them on the way.
4. **Done** — Confirm with a **4-digit code**, pay in cash, and rate the work. (Step 4 can fold into "Book"
   if a 3-step layout reads better; keep Post/Bid/Book as the hero triad.)

## 4. Feature highlights (locked product decisions — PRD §06, §03)

Pull from these; each is grounded. Pick the strongest for the Features section.

- **Name your need, pros bid.** The only bid-and-negotiate model in Pakistani home services — every
  incumbent fixes the price for you. *(This is THE differentiator — "the wedge." Lead with it.)*
- **Pros near you, matched by distance.** Jobs go to verified pros inside your area (proximity matching),
  so help is genuinely nearby.
- **Compare on more than price.** Every bid shows price, arrival time, rating, and distance — decide with
  full information.
- **Negotiate in chat.** Counter the offer until the price is right. The agreed price is locked in when you
  accept.
- **Ratings that stick.** Two-way ratings and reviews after every job build a track record that only exists
  on-platform.
- **Cash, paid directly.** You pay the pro in cash when the work is done — **Fauran never touches your cash.**
- **Built for Pakistan.** PKR-first, Lahore & Islamabad at launch, phone-number sign-up, works on everyday
  Android.

## 5. Trust section (explicit + prominent — PRD §01, §05, §09; deck slide 8)

Three pillars — this is the most important section for conversion:

1. **CNIC + selfie verified.** Every professional passes manual identity review — CNIC (front & back) plus a
   selfie face-match — before they can bid. Documents are stored encrypted, admin-only, never public.
   *(NADRA automated verification is a later phase — do NOT claim it is live.)*
2. **Prepaid wallet keeps pros accountable.** Pros fund a wallet before they can bid; the platform fee is
   taken from that wallet the moment a bid is accepted. It means only committed, real pros are in the pool —
   and it's why your **cash stays between you and the pro.**
3. **You hold the completion code.** A job only closes when **you** share a 4-digit code with the pro — no
   code, no close. You confirm the work is actually done before it's marked complete.

Supporting trust line: **"Fauran never touches your cash. You pay the pro directly, in full, when the work is
done."**

## 9. Tone

- Avoid: hype, fake urgency, unverifiable numbers, buzzwords.

## 10. Resolved tensions (READ — prevents ungrounded claims)

- **Escrow / "pay only when you approve":** The deck pitches escrow-by-default. The **PRD explicitly defers
  escrow to Phase 2** ("Fauran Guarantee") and states the MVP model is **cash paid directly to the pro**.
  → **Use the cash-direct + completion-code + prepaid-wallet model. Do NOT present escrow as a live feature.**
- **Categories:** Deck lists broad tasks (cleaning, moving, delivery, driving). The **PRD launch scope is
  skilled trades** (plumber, electrician, AC tech, carpenter, painter, handyman). App screenshots still show
  legacy broad tasks — ignore them for copy. → **Lead with skilled trades; "and more" is fine.**
- **Fee figures (7.5%, PKR 1,000–1,500/mo):** Unvalidated ("working assumptions, validated in Phase 0").
  → **Keep specific percentages/prices OFF the public site.** Describe the wallet mechanism qualitatively.
- **App screenshots** show an older hourly-rate / "submit actual hours" flow. → **Visual brand reference
  only** (green, logo, clean cards). Not a source for feature copy.
